Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

Doctor Error is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we handle it.

Data Collection

Doctor Error collects no personal data.

  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We do not collect IP addresses or device identifiers
  • We do not use cookies to track you across sessions
  • We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services

Error Submission

When you submit an error to Doctor Error for diagnosis, the error text is sent to our servers for analysis. This submission is anonymous and used only for:

  • Generating a diagnosis for your current session
  • Improving the accuracy and coverage of our diagnostic engine

We do not associate submitted errors with any personal identifier. Each submission is treated independently.

Cookies

Doctor Error uses only local storage to save your Pro token (if you unlock Pro). This is stored in your browser on your device and never transmitted to external services except Stripe for payment processing.

Third-Party Services

Doctor Error does not integrate with:

  • Google Analytics or similar analytics platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media tracking
  • Retargeting pixels

The only third-party integration is Stripe for Pro payment processing (if you choose to unlock Pro).

Your Data Rights

Since we collect no personal data, we have nothing to delete, export, or modify on your behalf. Your diagnostic sessions are private and used only for generating your current diagnosis.

Security

Submitted error text is transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted). We do not store submitted errors long-term or share them with any third party except as required by law.

Questions

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach out through the main website.
